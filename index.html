Welcome to the interactive trip data visualization dashboard! This page helps you analyze and explore taxi movement patterns in a dynamic and intuitive way, similar to how modern logistics or fleet management platforms operate.

How to Explore the Trip Data Visualization

Bar Chart - Taxi Activity Overview

The bar chart displays Taxi IDs on the X-axis and the number of trips on the Y-axis. Clicking on any bar will:

  • Highlight the selected taxi ID in orange.
  • Filter the map to show only the routes taken by that taxi.
  • Displays a pie chart summarizing trip durations for that taxi.
